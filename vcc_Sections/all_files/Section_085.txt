(5) MAS may, in its discretion, waive the payment of all or any part
of the remuneration and expenses mentioned in subsection (3).
(6) Where, in the course of an inspection under subsection (1),
MAS obtains any protected information as defined in section 17(1) of
the FSMA 2022, and that information is not necessary for taking any
action regarding non‑compliance with any direction issued or
regulation made under section 83 or 84, then MAS must treat that
information as secret.
